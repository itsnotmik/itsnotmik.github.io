About me

I am a Software Engineering that is focused on mainly Machine Learning. In January 2024, I joined Dr. Bilal Khan and an amazing team located at UCLA working on a reverse osmosis (RO) water treatment and desalination system. The research is funded by the US National Alliance for Water Innovation. I began working on developing a Graph Convolutional Neural network with attention mechanism (GATConv) for forcasting multi-modal operational performance of a live system installed in Northern Calfornia. We used the GATConv model to discern important causal relationships of multiple sensors for robust fault detection. I handled the in-depth data acquisition and cleaning, including outlier removal using violin plots and t-SNE.
